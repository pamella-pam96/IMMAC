About Codinex Computers Limited

Uganda's trusted ICT partner since 2012

Our Story

Empowering Uganda Through Technology

Codinex Computers Limited is an Information and Communication Technology (ICT) company located in Mbarara City, Uganda. The company was founded in 2012 by a team of dynamic Information Technology professionals and computer engineers.

Registered under the Registrar of Companies with Registration Number 152279, Codinex has built a strong reputation for providing reliable, innovative, and customer-oriented ICT solutions to individuals, businesses, and organizations across Uganda and the East African region.

With over 500 customers benefiting from our networking, cybersecurity, software development, and computer maintenance services, we continue to be a leading ICT company in Western Uganda.
